Our Story

Combining Comfort Food and Fine Mexican Cuisine

Founder and head chef Mike Fahrbach has always aspired to share meals with fellow friends and family, and provide the best food amongst every meal. His love for both comfort and Mexican cooking inspired him to branch out and take his home cooking to the public.

With the last name Fahrbach, Mike's friends created the nickname "FahrBQ" once he started to smoke various meats. Regardless that the name started off as a joke, it stuck and became the driving inspiration for him to open up a breakfast and lunch shop.

Family Owned and Operated

Opening in in Louisville in 2012, FahrBQ's is Chef Mike Fahrbach's way of passing on his contagious passion for cooking to his three children and close relatives.

Each location is owned and ran by one of his children. As the official owner and head chef, he chooses which restaurant to work from weekly.
